Learning to speak from the core of your being






De Nieuwe Stem works on three levels


Vision

Your voice tells the world who you are. And the more you are able to speak freely and authentically, the better the world will be able to hear you. But sometimes it can be difficult to use your voice in such a way that it accurately reflects who you are and what you want to say. By reconnecting your mind with your authentic self, you'll be able again to use your real voice and let the world know who you are.

By using your authentic voice freely, it becomes easier to be yourself. And this improved connection with your self, in turn, lets itself be heard in your voice. The ability to let your voice express who you really are is both empowering and liberating. Speaking freely, from your authentic self, makes it possible to really connect with people and for the world to hear your voice.

About

My name is Kyra Volleberg and I am the founder of De Nieuwe Stem. I have always been interested in the human voice and its ability to reveal things about its owner as well as its capacity to have powerful emotional effects. In order to learn more about the human voice as well as my own specifically, I have studied music and singing at the Royal Conservatory of Music in the Netherlands and at the Berklee School of Music. During my studies, I became interested in African-American culture and music, which I find deeply meaningful. I have completed an MA in (Afro-)American Studies and an MA in Education to further explore this interest and to develop the skills to share it.

The human voice has always been central to my interests. Looking for ways to allow the voice to express authentic identity, I have over the years collected techniques and insights from beyond conventional approaches to train the voice. I have used this collection to construct a training method that is centered on finding a balance between voice, breath and self. This method uses singing and vocal training but does not just aim to train a "good" singing voice. Instead, it uses breathing techniques and mindfulness to focus on clear and powerful speech, and understands persuasive speaking to be a function of finding strength in your authentic self. Using your voice to get to the core of you and using the core of you to learn how to speak: that is voice training at De Nieuwe Stem.
